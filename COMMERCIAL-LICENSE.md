## Which license do you need?

**Use the AGPL-3.0 (free) if:**
- You are using Arc Sentry for research, evaluation, or personal use.
- You are using it inside your organization without redistributing it or exposing it as part of a networked service.
- You are building an open source project that is itself AGPL-3.0 compatible.
- You are comfortable with the AGPL-3.0 requirement that any modifications or works that incorporate Arc Sentry — including works made available over a network — must also be released under AGPL-3.0.

**You need a commercial license if:**
- You want to embed Arc Sentry in a proprietary product or service.
- You are offering a service that uses Arc Sentry to customers and do not want to open-source your own code under AGPL-3.0.
- Your legal or compliance team will not approve AGPL-3.0 for internal use.
- You want a license that includes indemnification, a support SLA, or priority fixes.

If you are unsure which applies to your situation, email and ask.
